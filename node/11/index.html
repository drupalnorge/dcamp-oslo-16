What Really Changed with Drupal 8

Skrevet av dick.olsson ons, 10/19/2016 - 15:25
Session description

In this session I will talk about the future of Drupal 8 and some of the highlights of this new version. The Workflow Initiative is a topic I’ll explore in more detail, followed by a demo of what the future might look like with content management and workflow for Drupal.

The session will not be technical and focus mainly on users and organisations that wants to know more about Drupal 8 and its future.
